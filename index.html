Mr. Mostert

About

"Timo Mostert, head coach at American Fork High School in American Fork, Utah [the] National High School Boys’ Cross County Coach of the Year. Mostert began coaching the American Fork boys’ cross country team in 1998. His team has competed in every state championship since he took over the program, winning seven team championships. In 2016, Mostert led American Fork to its seventh team championship in the last eight years. In December, Mostert and his boys finished second at the Nike Cross Nationals. It was the second time in five years that American Fork finished runner-up at the national championship." nhsca.com
